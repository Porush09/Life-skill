# Focus Management

---

## Question 1: What is Deep Work?

Deep Work means focusing on important tasks without any distractions like emails, social media, or notifications.  
It involves hard thinking and problem-solving.  
This kind of work produces better results, higher creativity, and work that is hard for others to copy.

---

## Question 2: According to the author, how to do deep work properly? (Key Points)

1. **Schedule distraction‑free sessions**  
2. **Build a habit of deep work**  
3. **Develop a rhythm for deep work**  
4. **Prepare mentally and physically** for deep work  

---

## Question 3: How can you implement the principles in your day-to-day life?

1. **Fix time in your calendar** for deep work sessions  
2. **Build a distraction-free workspace**  
3. **Plan all tasks the night before**  
4. **Take breaks** to recharge your mind between sessions  
5. **Review your day** at the end to see what you accomplished  

---

## Question 4: What are the dangers of social media? (Brief)

1. Social media can be **addictive** and distract us from real life  
2. Can lead to **mental health issues**  
3. **False information** on social media can mislead us  
4. Sharing personal details can lead to **privacy breaches or misuse of data**
