## Question 3  
**What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?**

An internal locus of control is the belief that your own actions and efforts determine your outcomes, rather than luck or external factors. The key message of the video is that believing you control your results helps you stay motivated and persistent during difficulties.
