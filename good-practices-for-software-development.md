# My Learning Reflection

### Question 1: Which point(s) were new to you?

* I used to think I only needed to talk when someone asked me a question. Now I know it’s better to give updates even if they don't ask.
* I didn't take writing notes very seriously before. I see now that it helps a lot after a meeting is over.
* I learned that when I ask a question, I should also explain what I already tried to do first.
* I liked the idea of joining a call early just to chat with my team and get to know them better.

---

### Question 2: Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?

I think I need to work on talking to my team more and staying focused on my work. 
Sometimes I am shy to ask questions, or I wait too long to tell people if I am stuck. To get better, I will:

1.  **Speak up early:** If I am going to be late with a task, I will tell the team right away.
2.  **Write things down:** I will write down the project requirements so I don't forget them.
3.  **Put my phone away:** I will keep my phone in another spot so I don't get distracted while working.
4.  **Explain my problems clearly:** When I am stuck, I will explain exactly what I have already tried.
