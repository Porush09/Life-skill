# Question 1

## What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- By verbal behaviour like making sexual comments, sexual jokes and stories.
- By non-verbal behaviour like staring at someone or making sexual gestures or sending sexual pictures.
- Physical behaviour like unwanted touching, kissing or grabbing without consent.
- By stalking or sexual harassment via social media or chats.


# Question 2

## What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

- First remove yourself from danger, then if safe tell the harasser to stop and try to seek help from others nearby.
- Report to authorities or managers. Many institutions have Anti-Sexual Harassment Committees.
- If you witnessed harassment, support the victim and speak up if safe.
- Contact law enforcement if the behaviour is violent, images are shared without consent, or the person feels unsafe.
- Reach out to support services such as counselling and legal advice groups.
