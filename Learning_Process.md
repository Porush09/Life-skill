## Question 1: What is the Feynman Technique? (1 line)

The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you understand a concept deeply by explaining or teaching it in simple words.

## Question 3: What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

- **Active mode** is when you focus deeply and consciously work on a problem.  
- **Diffuse mode** is a relaxed state of mind that helps in forming new ideas and connections.

## Question 4: According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?

- Decide what you want to learn and set a clear goal  
- Break the topic into smaller parts  
- Learn just enough to start practicing  
- Avoid distractions while practicing  
- Practice consistently for at least 20 hours

## Question 5: What actions can you take to improve your learning process?

- Study with full focus and intentional practice  
- Keep the phone on silent and block social media during study time  
- Track time regularly to build discipline  
- Apply concepts through coding instead of only reading theory  
- Set clear deadlines and work towards achieving goals
